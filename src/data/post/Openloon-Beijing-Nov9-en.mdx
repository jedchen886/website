The OpenLoong University Tour visited Beijing University of Posts and Telecommunications (BUPT) and University of Science and Technology Beijing (USTB), bringing together experts from Zhiyuan Research Institute and GitCode to discuss the future of embodied intelligence, control frameworks, and open-source ecosystem development.

This collaborative event aimed to foster innovation and knowledge sharing among students, researchers, and industry professionals in the rapidly evolving field of robotics and AI.
